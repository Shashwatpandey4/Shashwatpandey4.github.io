These are some sample questions to find out insights about uber's taxi data :

What is the relationship between passenger count and trip distance? Do longer trips tend to have more passengers?

What is the relationship between payment type and fare amount? Do people who pay with credit cards typically pay more than people who pay with cash?

What is the relationship between time of day and fare amount? Are fares typically higher during rush hour?

What is the relationship between day of week and fare amount? Are fares typically higher on weekends?

What are the busiest hours for each pickup and dropoff location?
